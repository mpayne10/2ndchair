How It Works
Step 01
Brief the system
Before the hearing, your second chair enters case context: jurisdiction, case type, defense theory, key witnesses, and the legal issues you're watching. 90 seconds.

Capture the record
As testimony proceeds, the operator enters statements into the live transcript panel and tags each speaker. The record builds in real time alongside the proceedings.

Analyze on demand
Hit Analyze after any statement. Within seconds, objections, strategic angles, and case law surface — all filtered through your specific case context and jurisdiction.
